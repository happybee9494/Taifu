Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

Our 14-day free trial allows you to test the platform with up to 100 contacts and up to 100 emails sent. The features available within our trial match our Professional tier of features. If you are on a trial account, you won’t be charged unless you manually upgrade to a paid account — you can simply let your trial expire should you not want to continue. 

How much does ActiveCampaign cost?

ActiveCampaign pricing starts at $9 for accounts with less than 500 contacts. For more details on pricing, please visit our pricing page.

If you are a reseller, please visit our ActiveCampaign resellers page and use the discount calculator to calculate discount size and savings.  

How does ActiveCampaign compare against other tools?

If you'd like to see what sets ActiveCampaign apart from other email marketing, marketing automation, and CRM tools, please visit our comparison page.

What payment methods do you accept?

Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. For annual plans we also accept Paypal, checks, and wire transfers.

Do I need a lot of technical experience to use ActiveCampaign?

You don’t need to be a developer or a designer to use ActiveCampaign. Anyone can create email marketing campaigns and marketing automations on our platform. Our Automation Recipe Library contains hundreds of pre-built automations — no coding skills are required. If you do find yourself needing some help, feel free to reach out to our support team through our Contact page, or visit our Support Center.

Can you help me migrate from another service?

Yes, we offer free migration services! Based on the plan you choose, we will:

  • Import your lists, contacts, custom fields, and tags
  • Recreate opt-in forms
  • Recreate your automation workflows
  • Recreate email templates

For additional information, please visit our Migration page.
