Frequently Asked Questions

How does the subscription work? When will I be charged?
The subscription will automatically charge you at the end of your billing period (either annual or monthly) to maintain your subscription, but you can cancel at any time.

How do I manage my subscription if I don’t want to be on an auto-renew plan?
If you don't see a Manage Subscription button, that means you are not on an auto-renew plan. You won't be charged unless you subscribe to a new plan.

What if I tried Premium and didn’t like it? Can I get a refund?
We offer a 14-day refund policy on annual subscriptions if you are not happy with your purchase. Please email us at info@toodledo.com. We do not offer refunds on our monthly subscriptions.
