This is a very beta feature. It's intended mostly as a novelty right now. It has undergone almost no testing yet, and there's a lot of room for improvement.

If an article is not included, it's most likely because Instapaper didn't feel confident enough in its text-only parse of the page, which caused it to leave too many extraneous elements in the page such as navigation links or forms.

Finally, please be mindful of your paper usage. Use this feature sparingly, only print what you'll read, and please recycle the paper when you're done.
